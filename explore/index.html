The Real Kashmir

Wonders within reach

Muzaffarabad is the doorway to some of Pakistan's most beautiful country. From emerald rivers to alpine meadows, here is where the valley will take you, and we are glad to arrange the car, jeep, or guide to get you there.

60 km · Day trip

Neelum Valley

The jewel of Azad Kashmir, emerald water, wooden villages, and mountains that climb to the sky.

30 km · Half day

Peer Chinasi

A revered hilltop shrine wrapped in cloud, with views across the whole Muzaffarabad basin.

40 km · Day trip

Ganga Choti

Rolling green summits above Bagh, cool air, wildflowers, and endless walking country.

50 km · Day trip

Bagh

A green district of terraced hills and quiet towns on the road deeper into the ranges.

80 km · Overnight

Lepa Valley

One of the most beautiful and least-visited valleys, famed for its timber homes and orchards.

Walking distance

Food Street & City

Muzaffarabad's riverside food street, bazaars, and the confluence of the Neelum and Jhelum.
