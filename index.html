Welcome

A gracious riverside home in the heart of the valley

Set along the banks of the Neelum in Muzaffarabad, Hotel 1954 pairs the serenity of Azad Kashmir with the quiet comforts of a boutique retreat. Every room opens to a private balcony where the mountains meet the river.

From the first cup of Kashmiri chai to the last still evening on the terrace, we host families and travellers the way the valley always has, warmly, and without pretense.
