The Beginning

Born of the valley, built for guests

Hotel 1954 began with a simple wish: to give travellers a place in Muzaffarabad that felt less like a hotel and more like being welcomed into a Kashmiri home. Since first opening our doors in 2016, we have hosted families, couples, and explorers from across Pakistan and beyond.

Set in Tanveer Koul Plaza along the Lower Plate, we sit where the town meets the water, a short walk from the food street, and a breath away from the mountains that draw everyone to this valley.

The Setting

Where the town meets the mountains

Muzaffarabad is the gateway to Azad Kashmir, the last stop before the roads climb into Neelum Valley, Peer Chinasi, and beyond. From our balconies you can watch the river run and the peaks change colour through the day.

We are moments from the city centre and the celebrated food street, yet far enough to keep the evenings quiet and the mornings still.
